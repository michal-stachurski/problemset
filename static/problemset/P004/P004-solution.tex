$$ \vec{a} = (a_1,a_2) \qquad \vec{b} = (b_1,b_2) $$
Iloczyn skalarny:
$$ \vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = a_1 b_1 + a_2 b_2 $$
$$ \vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = \vec{b} \cdot \vec{a} $$
$$ \vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = |\vec{b}| \cdot |\vec{a}| \cdot \cos \theta $$

Iloczyn wektorowy:
$$ \vec{a} \times \vec{b} = a_1 b_2 - a_2 b_1 $$
$$ \vec{a} \times \vec{b} = -\vec{b} \times \vec{a} $$
$$ \vec{a} \times \vec{b} = |\vec{b}| \cdot |\vec{a}| \cdot \sin \theta $$
